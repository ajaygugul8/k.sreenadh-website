K.Sreenadh

Professor

Department of Mathematics and Computing, IIT Delhi

Biosketch

I embarked on my academic journey with a Ph.D. in Mathematics from IIT Kanpur in 2002, leading to diverse experiences across prestigious institutions globally. After post-doctoral stints at TIFR, Bangalore, and the University of Toulouse, France, I joined IIT Delhi as an Assistant Professor in 2004.

Despite an enticing offer from IISER Bhopal in 2009, I opted to continue at IIT Delhi, eventually becoming a Professor in 2017. Recognized for my contributions, I assumed the Institute Chair Professor role in 2019, holding it until 2023, when I was honored as Professor (HAG).

Currently, as I continue my academic journey at IIT Delhi, holding the esteemed position of Professor (HAG), my dedication extends to mentoring the next generation of scholars. I eagerly anticipate the ongoing pursuit of knowledge, collaborative ventures with peers, and the shared joy that academic exploration brings.

Area of interests

  • Partial Differential Equations: Theory of Elliptic & Parabolic equations
  • Numerical Analysis: Finite element analysis
